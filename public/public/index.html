How can you make a magic square using your date of birth?

There are two methods to make a magic square

  • First method is shown below.
  • step 1:First take a 4*4 matrix and write your birth date as mentioned above in the first row of the matrix.
  • step 2:Now write the same numbers u have written in the first row again but in the reverse order in the second row.
  • step 3:Fill the first two places of the third row with the numbers written in the first two places of first row but in reverse order.And the remaining two places should be filled with the numbers written in last two places of the first row but in reverse order.
  • step 4:Fill the first two places of the fourth row with numbers written in the first two places of the second row but in reverse order.And the remaining two places should be filled with numbrts written in the last two places of the second row but in reverse order.
  • step 5:Now take another 4*4 matrix in which it's first row should be filled with zeroes,second row should be filled with 1,-1,-3,+3 respectively,third row should be filled with -2,+2,+2,-2 respectively,fourth row should be filled with -1,+1,-1,+1 respectively
  • step 6:Now add both the matrices the result will be a magic square which is formed using your date of birth.
